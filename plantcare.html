Trailing Plants Trailing plants grow vines of leaves that are commonly kept in hanging baskets for a dramatic effect.

Also known as devil's ivy, a healthy pothos can grow hundreds of feet of leafy vines. They come in a variety of colors and patterns.

Fiddle Leaf Figs are picky about their sunlight. If they find a spot in the sun where they thrive, they do not like to be moved.
